Making a Presentation

Once you have used Transana to transcribe and analyze your data, the final step is to share that data with others. To facilitate this, Transana has a presentation mode.

In presentation mode, Transana can use the full screen to play a video, or display only the video window (across the top of the screen) and the transcript window (across the bottom).

Activity: To use presentation mode, follow these directions.

1. On the Options menu, click Presentation mode. Then click Video Only.

2. Double-click the name of a clip (Houses, for example) and click the play button on the Video window control bar. Transana uses the entire monitor to play the clip. Notice the control bar is still visible at the bottom of the screen, so that you can stop or restart the clip as necessary. When the clip finishes playing, the other windows return.

Try selecting Video and Transcript Only instead of Video Only. Notice the transcript window at the bottom of the screen. To return to regular operation, on the Options menu, click Presentation mode, then click All Windows.
