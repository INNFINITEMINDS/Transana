Converting Search Results to Collections

Transana lets you preserve the results of a search as a Collection. When you discover something brilliant using the Search in Transana, one way to save that analytic insight is through creating a Collection full of the data items that support the new insight.

In this activity you convert the results of the Clip adn Quick Creation Method search into a Collection called Quick Clip Search Results.

Activity: Follow these directions:

1. Right-click the search result named Clip and Quick Creation Method. It's shown in the database tree with a magnifying glass icon next to it. Select Convert to Collection from the pop-up menu.

2. Transana opens the Collection Properties dialog box. Replace Clip and Quick Creation Method with Quick Clip Search Results in the field labeled Collection ID. Add your initials to the field labeled Owner and select Tutorial as the Default Keyword Group. Click OK when you have finished.

Notice that the Search Result disappears when the conversion is complete. Also note that Transana will not convert Documents or Episodes if they are included in the search, because Documents and Episodes cannot be included in Collections even though they can be included in Search Results.

3. Finally, create a Collection Note to document the process by which you reached your brilliant analytic insight.
