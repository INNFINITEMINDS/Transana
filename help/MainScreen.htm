Main Screen

When you start Transana, you will see the Transana menu bar and four windows: the video window, the transcript window, the data window, and the visualization window.

The video window is where video is displayed.

The transcript window is where a transcript is entered, displayed and edited.

The data window is where you view, organize, and manipulate Transana data objects other than video files and transcriptions.

The visualization window displays visual representations of the data, in the form of a waveform diagram representing the audio track of your video, or of a bar chart showing the coding that has been applied to the video during the analytic process.
