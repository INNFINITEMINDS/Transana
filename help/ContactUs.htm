Contact Us

Please direct all inquiries, questions, and bug reports about Transana to:

http://www.transana.org

Specifically, questions should be posted to the Transana User Forum at http://www.transana.org/forum. If your question is not appropriate for the forums, please use the Contact form on the web site at http://www.transana.org/contact.htm.

Transana is currently being expanded and maintained by David Woods of the Wisconsin Center for Education Research at the University of Wisconsin, Madison. The project was originally conceived and started by Chris Fassnacht to facilitate a Conversation Analysis for his dissertation.
