About Waveforms

If this is the first time the sample video has been loaded on your computer, you see the following prompt:

Click Yes. Transana creates a waveform of the video. A waveform is a visual representation of the intensity of the video's sound. Once generated, the waveform is stored in a file. Every time you view the video, Transana displays the waveform in the Visualization window.
