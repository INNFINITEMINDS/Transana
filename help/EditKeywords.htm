Edit Keywords

To add Keywords to a Document, Episode, Quote, Clip, or whole Snapshot from this dialog box, select the appropriate Keyword Group in the first column, then either double-click the desired Keyword in the second column, or click the Keyword once and press the  button.

To remove a Keyword, highlight the Keyword Group : Keyword pair in the Keywords column and click the  button.

To add Keywords or Keyword Groups to the list of defined Keywords that can be assigned to a Document, Episode, Quote, Clip, or Snapshot, press the  button. This will load the Keyword Management dialog.
