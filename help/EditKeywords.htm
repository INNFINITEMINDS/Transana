Edit Keywords

To add keywords to an Episode or Clip from this window, select the appropriate Keyword Group in the first window, then either double-click the desired keyword or click the keyword once and press the  button.

To remove a keyword, highlight the Keyword Group : Keyword pair in the Keywords window and click the  button.

To add keywords or keyword groups to the list of defined keywords that can be assigned to an Episode or Clip, press the  button.
